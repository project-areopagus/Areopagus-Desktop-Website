Who It's For

Built for people who care about their community.

Whether you're building an audience, managing a community, or developing the next great integration — Areopagus is your platform.

Your community, your livelihood

Stop splitting yourself across Discord, Patreon, and link trees. Areopagus brings your community and your supporters into one place — with a 5% take rate that leaves more in your pocket.

  • Subscriptions, exclusive content, and digital product sales — all native
  • 5% take rate — half of what Patreon charges
  • Creator analytics and earnings dashboard

Privacy by default

No facial recognition, no ID verification, no data harvesting. Areopagus gives your community private spaces where conversations stay between members — not in a corporation's database.

  • AES-256 encryption on every message and call
  • Zero-knowledge anonymity layer — even we can't identify you
  • No ads, no trackers, no algorithmic feeds

An open platform

The open-source core and plugin architecture mean you can build exactly the features your community needs. Contribute upstream, publish plugins, or fork the whole thing.

For Organizations

Private infrastructure for teams that take security seriously.

Deploy Areopagus for your organization and keep every conversation, file, and call under your control.

Self-hosted deployment

Run Areopagus on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your network. Full admin control over permissions, retention policies, and compliance.

Admin dashboard

Manage users, monitor channels, set role-based access controls, and audit communication logs — all from a centralized panel built for IT teams.

SSO and compliance

SAML/OIDC single sign-on, SCIM user provisioning, and audit-ready logging. Designed for SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR environments from day one.

Priority support

Dedicated onboarding, a private support channel, and an SLA that guarantees response times. Your team gets direct access to the engineers who build the product.
